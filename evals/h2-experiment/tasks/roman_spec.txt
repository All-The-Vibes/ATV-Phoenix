Implement the function to_roman(n) which converts an integer from 1 to 3999 inclusive to a Roman numeral. Use standard subtractive notation where 4 is IV, 9 is IX, 40 is XL, 90 is XC, 400 is CD, 900 is CM. Larger numerals come first and you greedily subtract. It must be valid for all n from 1 to 3999. For example 4 gives IV. 49 gives XLIX. 444 gives CDXLIV. 1994 gives MCMXCIV. 3999 gives MMMCMXCIX.
